I'm not a bug hunter, but I do occasionally find and report vulnerabilities as
part of my job.

## ICS-ALERT-20-217-01

[ROS Industrial Robot Motion Servers (KUKA and ABB)](https://rosindustrial.org/news/2020/6/23/how-to-securely-control-your-robot-with-ros-industrial) insufficient verification of data autenticity leading to arbitrary command execution or denial of service. More details and mitigation at [ICS-ALERT-20-217-01](https://us-cert.cisa.gov/ics/alerts/ics-alert-20-217-01).
